This kind of gradient decent helps us decrease the amount of computation we do per epoch. if you think about it this makes scene because when we descending down the slope all we need to do is go down😂, we don't need to find the most accurate mathematical direction. even if we deviate a bit from the true down direction it doesn't matter that much.

The reason stochastic gradient descent doesn't usually find the most accurate down direction its because we  use a sample of our data to calculate the direction of our next epoch. the is good because we sampled correctly our sample should resemble out data thus also giving us a down direction.
